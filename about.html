Background & Education

Hello! I am Wan Faruq Jazli bin Wan Ahmad Jaffry. I am the third child in my family and I come from Chukai, Terengganu.

My passion for technology started early; I began learning Computer Science in Form 4 at SMK Chukai. I later furthered my studies in Physical Science at Kolej Matrikulasi Johor (KMJ), and I am now currently majoring in Bachelor of Computer Science (Data Engineering) at UTM.
